Chicken and Broccoli

Description

This is a quick and dirty favorite of mine. It requires very little know-how.

My girlfriend is aghast at my cooking. I'm essentially making meat hot and vegetables soft then burying the result in hot sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. Chicken Breast
  • 1 lbs. Frozen Broccoli
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Hot Sauce of choice

Steps

  1. Heat the oil on medium-high heat in a large skillet
  2. Cut the chicken breast into large chunks and season liberally with S&P
  3. Cook the chicken until there's just a little pink in the middle
  4. Add the frozen broccoli
  5. Put on lid and cook until the broccoli is soft
  6. Check to make sure the chicken is cooked all the way through. No pink!
  7. Plate the food and obliterate it with hot sauce
